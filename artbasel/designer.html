ABOUT EXA SWIM SHOWS

Since 2020, EXA Models has been a premier source of elite talent, providing models for top fashion shows, campaigns, editorials, and brand launches.

In 2022, EXA began producing its own shows — quickly gaining attention for high-impact productions where swimwear fashion meets art. From Miami Swim Week to Art Basel and NYFW, EXA curates top international designers, world-class models, and live performances into unforgettable runway moments.

Art Basel Miami 2025 — EXA returns to Miami's most iconic cultural event, providing designers with unparalleled exposure at the intersection of fashion and art.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the application requirements?

Applicants must have a completed collection (minimum 5 looks), professional lookbook or portfolio images, brand story/designer bio, and clear creative vision. We're looking for innovative swimwear, resort wear, and beachwear designs that align with EXA's aesthetic of bold, fashion-forward style.

What's included in each package?

All packages include runway time, professional photography, video coverage, and social media promotion. Featured Designers receive 15-20 runway looks, 50+ professional photos, highlight reels, and premium marketing. Emerging Designers get 10-15 looks, 30+ photos, and social features. New Talent receives 5-10 looks, 20+ photos, and social tags. All designers keep full rights to their content.

When is the application deadline?

Application deadline is October 15, 2025 for maximum visibility in pre-event marketing. Designer selections will be announced by October 30, 2025. Final collection details and model casting must be completed by November 20, 2025. Event dates: December 3-6, 2025 during Art Basel Miami Beach.

Can I customize my package?

Yes! We offer flexible packages tailored to your specific needs. Whether you want additional runway time, extended photo/video coverage, exclusive after-party activation, or special brand integrations, we'll work with you to create the perfect showcase experience. Contact us to discuss custom options.

What type of designs are accepted?

We're focused on swimwear, resort wear, and beachwear that pushes creative boundaries. We welcome bold prints, innovative cuts, sustainable materials, and artistic concepts. Both established brands and emerging designers are encouraged to apply. Your collection should reflect quality craftsmanship and a distinct point of view.

How many designers will be featured?

We feature 10-15 designers per show to maintain exclusivity and ensure each collection receives meaningful attention. This curated approach allows us to give proper runway time, media coverage, and promotional focus to every participating designer.
