SWIM SHOWS

Since 2020, EXA Models has been a premier source of elite talent, providing models for top fashion shows, campaigns, editorials, and brand launches.

In 2022, EXA began producing its own shows — quickly gaining attention for high-impact productions where swimwear fashion meets art. From Miami Swim Week to Art Basel and NYFW, EXA curates top international designers, world-class models, and live performances into unforgettable runway moments.

Coming February 2026, EXA brings its signature show experience to Hawaii — expanding its reach and continuing to redefine the fashion event landscape.

The Hawai'i Experience

🏖️ Island Paradise Venue

Our February 2026 shows will take place at stunning Hawaii locations that capture the spirit of the islands—where ocean views meet high fashion. Expect intimate beachside venues, luxury resorts, and curated spaces that bring together the best of Hawaii's natural beauty and world-class production quality.

🌺 Cultural Respect & Stewardship

We partner with local talent and follow community guidance to ensure a respectful, positive impact. Our events honor Hawaiian culture and traditions, working closely with local artists, performers, and businesses to create an authentic experience that celebrates the islands' rich heritage.

🌱 Sustainability Pledge

EXA is committed to environmentally conscious events. We implement plastic-free backstage operations, partner with local sustainable vendors, and prioritize eco-friendly production practices. Our goal is to create stunning fashion experiences while protecting the beautiful islands that host us.

Frequently Asked Questions

📸 What content rights do sponsors receive?

Sponsors receive full usage rights to all content from their dedicated shoots and activations. This includes professional photos, video footage, and behind-the-scenes content. Ultimate and Gold sponsors also receive edited reels and social-ready content packages. All content can be used across your marketing channels indefinitely.

📦 What deliverables are included in each package?

Each tier includes specific deliverables: Ultimate sponsors receive 3+ social posts, dedicated photoshoots, VIP access, and branded highlight reels. Gold sponsors get 1-2 social posts, model content shoots, and event recognition. Silver sponsors receive group thank-you posts and program listings. All packages include logo placement on event materials and digital promotion appropriate to the tier.

🎟️ How many complimentary tickets are included?

Ultimate sponsors receive 8 VIP tickets with backstage access. Gold sponsors receive 4 VIP tickets. Silver sponsors receive 2 general admission tickets. Venue partners receive custom ticket allocations based on their tier. Additional tickets can be purchased at a discounted rate for all sponsors.

📅 What are the key deadlines?

Sponsorship commitment deadline is December 15, 2025 for maximum visibility in pre-event marketing. Logo and brand assets due by January 5, 2026. Final activation details must be confirmed by January 20, 2026. Event dates: February 2026 (exact dates TBD).

💰 What is the payment and refund policy?

50% deposit due upon signing, remaining balance due 30 days before the event. We offer flexible payment plans for larger sponsorship packages. Cancellations made 60+ days before the event receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 60 days are non-refundable, but sponsorship can be transferred to a future EXA event with approval.

🎪 Can we customize our sponsorship package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom activations tailored to your brand's objectives. Whether you want to add influencer meet-and-greets, after-party presenting sponsorship, VIP cabanas, or unique brand experiences, we'll work with you to design the perfect partnership. Contact us to discuss custom packages.

📊 How do you measure and report ROI?

All sponsors receive a comprehensive post-event report including: total impressions, engagement metrics, press coverage summary, social media reach, content performance analytics, and high-resolution photo/video deliverables. Ultimate and Gold sponsors also receive influencer partnership data and web traffic referral analysis.

🏝️ Why sponsor the Hawaii show specifically?

The Hawaii show offers a unique opportunity to align your brand with paradise, wellness, and luxury lifestyle positioning. The island setting attracts affluent travelers, resort brands, and lifestyle-focused media. It's ideal for brands in beauty, wellness, resort wear, travel, hospitality, and luxury products looking to connect with a high-net-worth, vacation-minded audience.
